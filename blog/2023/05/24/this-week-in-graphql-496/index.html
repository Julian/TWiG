Hello and welcome to another issue of This Week in GraphQL! GraphQL is a query language for APIs and a runtime for fulfilling those queries with your existing data. This is a weekly summary of its progress and community. Want something mentioned? Tag us at @ThisWeekInGraphQL on Twitter or @ThisWeekinGraphQL on mastodon.social, or send us a pull request.
